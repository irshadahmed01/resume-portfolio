Introduction to Google Sheets

In this, we are going to learn about Google Sheets. Google Sheets are part of Google. You can use Google Sheets to create powerful spreadsheets, even complex ones. These sheets are online in nature. You can create, edit, and collaborate whenever and wherever you want. You can choose a template or create one from the template gallery.

You can upload files too, like from Excel or Apple Numbers, etc.

Each cell can hold so much info—it could be numbers or alphabets. We can add sheets by clicking on the + to add more sheets. A bunch of those sheets in a single file is called a workbook.
